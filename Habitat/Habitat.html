Pangolin Habitats

A Bit of Biology

Pangolins are mamals of the Pholidota order. The one surviving family of this order is known as Manidae which consists of 3 genera:

  • Manis
  • Phataginus
  • Smutsia

The Manis refers to the genus that contains South Asian, and East Asian pangolins.
